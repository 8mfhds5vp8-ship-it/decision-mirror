within the project.

## Responsibilities

Maintainers:

- Approve governance changes
- Enforce charter
- Resolve conflicts
- Maintain documentation integrity

---

## Appointment

New maintainers require:

- Public nomination
- 2/3 consensus
- 30-day review period

---

## Removal

Removal requires:

- Documented cause
- Community notice
- Consensus vote

---

## Term

Maintainer positions are reviewed annually.

Maintainers may step down voluntarily at any time.
